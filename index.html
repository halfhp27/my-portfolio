UX & Growth Web Product Tridge · B2B Tech · South Korea

Redesigning the User Journey to Reverse High Bounce Rates and Drive Engagement

We discovered that users were leaving the website before exploring Tridge’s products leading to high bounce rate.

Problem
High bounce rates indicated users were not finding what they expected on arrival which signals misalignment between traffic intent, landing page messaging, and navigation architecture.
Hypothesis
Restructuring the navigation hierarchy and rewriting the homepage around clearer user journeys would reduce friction and increase time-on-site and engagement.
Execution
Conducted user session analysis and reviews to identify drop-off patterns. Collaborated with front-end developers to redesign the homepage layout, simplify navigation, and realign CTAs to user intent. Ran iterative tests on key landing pages before full rollout.
Results
Achieved 11% increase in user engagement.
↑ Engagement
Product Launch Monetization Lazada · E-Commerce Platform · Philippines

Launching Two CPC Ad Solutions to Unlock Merchant Revenue — New Product Launcher & First Search Slot

Merchants using Lazada needed better solutions/tools to drive traffic and sales for new listings. We identified the monetization gap, built the go-to-market strategy, and launched two CPC products that increased both seller adoption and platform revenue.

Problem
Merchants launching new products struggled with discoverability. The platform had no dedicated CPC solutions to boost new listings or guarantee high-visibility search placements, leaving potential additional revenue unrealized.
Hypothesis
Two targeted CPC products, one for boosting new product listings’ visibility, one for premium search placement, would address the issue in discoverability, increasing seller adoption and ad revenue simultaneously.
Execution
Managed full product roadmap for CPC solutions. Collaborated with engineering on UX/UI improvements. Crafted user education guides and onboarding materials. Executed email and in-app message campaigns targeting the right merchant segments. Conducted sales team trainings to drive adoption and oversaw end-to-end launch timelines.
Results
New Product Launcher increased merchant adoption rates and First Search Slot increased gross revenue.
↑ ARPU ↑ Adoption
Strategic Pivot Revenue Recovery Globe Telecom · Telecom · Philippines

Reversing Pandemic-Driven Revenue Collapse by Launching Zoom as a Core B2B Service

When COVID-19 forced most offices to shift to a work-from-home setup, Globe's B2B voice revenue was in danger. Rather than defend a declining product, we identified an opportunity, secured a distribution partnership with Zoom, and generated a new major source of revenue for the voice business.

Problem
Business customers suspended their subscription to traditional voice services as government-mandated lockdowns emptied offices. The revenue base was rapidly decreasing. Legacy voice products could not serve a remote-first business environment.
Hypothesis
Repositioning the Voice portfolio to include cloud collaboration tools (specifically Zoom), which businesses were starting to seek, would capture the demand shift and more than offset the voice revenue decline.
Execution
Negotiated and formalized the Zoom distribution partnership. Built a comprehensive internal product playbook to enable rapid sales team onboarding. Created the service-level agreement framework for issue escalation. Designed bundling and promotional offers with existing products to maximize uptake. Managed back-end operational processes to ensure delivery readiness.
Results
Successfully launched Zoom on time and achieved 105% of FY2020 annual revenue target despite the pandemic.
105% Revenue Target 6 Consecutive Quarters with +EBITDA
Pricing Strategy Market Expansion LG Electronics · Consumer Electronics · Philippines

Achieving Highest Profit Growth in APAC Through Structured Pricing Strategy and Demand Forecasting

Facing an aggressive profit growth target, I restructured the pricing and go-to-market strategy for LG's audio portfolio, moving from reactive promotions to a hypothesis-driven, data-validated approach that unlocked both volume and margin expansion.

Problem
Despite meeting prior-year targets, an aggressive new annual sales goal required strategy improvements. Pricing was reactive to competition, demand forecasting required more precision, and premium model adoption was constrained by purchase accessibility.
Hypothesis
A data-driven pricing strategy that's built on market research, competitor benchmarking, and channel-specific promotional programs, combined with rigorous demand forecasting to eliminate inventory inefficiencies, would drive both unit volume and margin simultaneously.
Execution
Conducted structured market research and competitive analysis to define product positioning and pricing. Designed nationwide and per-channel dealer programs to increase both market share and in-store conversion. Built demand forecasting models using historical sales trends. Managed the product roadmap, selecting which models to launch, retain, or phase out, and deployed financing promotions to increase premium model accessibility.
Results
+21% units sold YoY. 100% sales target achievement. 30% profit growth — the highest among all LG subsidiaries in APAC that year. Long-term inventory below 2% company benchmark.
+30% YoY Profit #1 Profit Growth in APAC 100% Sales Target Achievement
